WHAT IS AUDIOMATION

AUDIOMATION is a interactive design project combining music and animaiton. it works on the concept of triggers. triggers is a name given to particualr hotkeys that trigger a sound and an animation. you can assign what trigger will play what sound as well as what animation. you can create collections of triggers that are grouped into kits. there are eight triggers per kit and four kits avaiable at a single time. there are two types of kits, audio & visual. these kits can be saved and loaded when you deciede to visit again!

THE SEQUENCER

The next major part of audiomation is the sequencer. You turn this on with with the () key. this starts a track head that loops after a set amount of time. When you play a note it will log it to the sequencer and play that note again when the track head starts the loop over.

HOT KEYS

Hot key is a term for any key on the key board that will have input into audiomation. This includes triggers, x, y, & z.

EFFECTS

efffects are different distortions that you can apply to any of the animations found with in the animation grid.
